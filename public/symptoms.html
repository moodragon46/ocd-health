What are symptoms of OCD?

  • Obsessive need for cleanliness
  • Exaggerated fear of sickness or contamination
  • Needing for placements to be in patterns
  • Repeatedly counting items
  • Hoarding items
  • Obsessive fear of harm to oneself or to others
  • Continuously checking in case of forgetting items
  • If religious, feeling the need to pray countless times a day
  • Also having other disorders such as
    1. Anxiety
    2. Depression
    3. Eating disorders
    4. Other similar disorders
